Dropdowns

Use the ng-bootstrap ngbDropdown component with ngDropdownToggle and ngbDropdownMenu for dropdowns

Positioning
Bottom Left

Dropdowns are positioned to bottom with alignment to left by default

Bottom Right

Use placement="bottom-right" to align dropdown to right

Top Left

Use placement="top-left" for a dropup Menu aligned to left

Top Right

Use placement="top-right" for a dropup Menu aligned to right
